About Me

Introduction

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Mathematics at the University of Copenhagen. My research interests span across multiple domains, with a particular focus on the intersection of mathematics, statistics, and computational methods.

Research Interests

My work centers on understanding causal relationships and developing robust statistical methods:

  • Causal Inference: Developing and applying methods to understand cause-and-effect relationships in complex systems
  • Causal Discovery: Algorithmic approaches to learning causal structures from observational data
  • High-Dimensional Statistics: Statistical methods for analyzing data with many variables
  • Narrative Systems: Exploring the mathematical structure of narratives and storytelling
  • Digital Humanities: Applying computational and statistical methods to humanities research
